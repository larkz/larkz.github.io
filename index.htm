About

The TUM Canadian Students Association, is an accredited student association founded at the University of Technical University of Munich.

The group is formed via a gathering of like minded individuals with an interest in Canadian-German issues, and wish to meet other individuals with the same interests both here at TUM and in the overarching community.

Anyone can become a member of this student group. Simply send your application to the President of the TUM-CAS (larkin.liu@tum.de) writing via email or post. There are no restrictions on membership. There is no membership fee.
